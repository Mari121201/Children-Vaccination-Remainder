About Children Vaccination Remainder

Childhood immunization is crucial for protecting children from serious and potentially life-threatening diseases such as Measles, polio, Tuberculosis, Hepatitis B, Diphtheria, Pertussis (Whooping cough), Tetanus and more. Despite the availability of national health programs like the National Health Mission (NHM) aimed at newborn and child health, vaccination rates often fall short of optimal levels globally. This shortfall underscores the ongoing public health risk posed by vaccine-preventable diseases.

Our project aims to address this challenge by providing a streamlined solution for parents and caregivers to manage and track their child’s vaccination schedule effectively. Leveraging the power of modern technology, particularly SMS (Short Message Service), our children vaccination remainder model ensures that parents receive timely notifications about upcoming vaccinations.

How it works

1. Input child's details

Begin by entering yoru child's information into our easy-to-use interface. Include details such as your child's name, date of birth, and any previous vaccination history if available

2. Set up vaccination schedule

Once the initial details are entered our system will help you set up a personalized vaccination schedule based on recommended immunization guidelines. You can input the dates of past vaccinations and upcoming due dates

3. Receive timely notifications

Our system utilizes SMS (Short Message Service) to send you automatic reminders before each scheduled vaccination. These reminders ensure you never miss a crucial immunization date for your child

4. Stay informed and updated

Stay informed about the latest advancements in childhood immunization through our platform. We provide updates on new vaccination programs, research findings, and health tips to support your child's well-being

5. Access anytime, anywhere

Our platform is designed to be accessible from any device with internet access. Whether you're at home or on the go, you can easily check and update vaccination schedules
